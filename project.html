Recent Projects

Hungry Dolphin

Hungry Dolphin is a game where players control a dolphin by using the left and right arrow keys to move it across the bottom of the screen. Strawberries fall from the top, and the goal is to collect as many as possible. As your score increases, the strawberries fall faster. However, if a strawberry reaches the ground before the dolphin catches it, the game ends!

Flappy Bird

Flappy Bird is a side-scrolling game where players control a bird by pressing the spacebar or the up arrow key to guide it between columns of green pipes. If the bird hits a pipe, the sky, or the ground, the game is over!

Vehicle Simulation

Vehicle Simulation is a simulation featuring four vehicles—a motorcycle, a car, a bus, and an ambulance—each moving at different speeds: the motorcycle is the fastest, followed by the car, then the bus, and the ambulance, which is the slowest. These vehicles drive down the street while pedestrians, including a person and an elephant, attempt to cross. If a motorcycle or car hits the person, they fall and remain on the ground, but if the bus comes into contact with them, it picks them up. The ambulance heals a fallen person if it comes into contact with them. The elephant, however, is unaffected by any of the vehicles. Additionally, a tornado can randomly appear, wiping out the entire road and everything on it.
